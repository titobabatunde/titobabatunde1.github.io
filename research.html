Generating DNA Origami Nanostructures through Shape Annealing

B Babatunde, DS Arias, J Cagan, RE Taylor

This article is the first step in addressing current design limitations by computing optimal scaffold routing only with a set of constraints and desired configurations. This approach optimally directs the growth of a continuous scaffold strand along a honeycomb lattice architecture by dividing the scaffold into 7bp sections. This ultimately broadens the design space and generates complex multilayer DNA origami designs.
